About Me

Makayla Colonello is a creative and an avid learner. She is a classically trained pianist and graduated from Berklee College of Music in 2021, studying Music Business Management and Songwriting. Following her undergrad degree, she is currently in the MIT xPRO Professional Certificate in Coding program. Makayla's goals are to combine both music and software development. She sees the two as connected instead of separated and hopes to bring her musician's lense into the world of software development.
